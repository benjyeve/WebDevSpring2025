My Videos

Mando Hates Dubstep (2020)

Created after the season 2 finale of the Mandalorian, I combined aspects of the episode with a royalty-free dubstep beat as a callback to a prior viral Star Wars video, "The Techno Union". Video made in Adobe Premiere.

Future Casting Project (2020)

An experimental video created for a class project. Focusing on a hypothetical fullbody feedback VR suit, the suit was modeled in Blender before being exported into Unity. Recording took place in VRChat before editing in Adobe Premiere.

The Two Most Controversial Rules in Rehydrated Speedrunning History (2020)

A video documenting two rules within the Rehydrated speedrunning community. All footage taken with permission from the original owners, I tried replicating a similar style to others who have created informative documentary-style videos within the speedrunning community. Edited in Adobe Premiere.

VR Twins (2020)

A video toying around the premise of a person's actions suddenly being hijacked via a third party, similarly to "Being John Malkovich". Recorded remotely during the COVID-19 quarantine, a unique challenge arose where my friend and I had to coordinate remotely to understand what types of shots had to be filmed and how the final product will turn out. Edited in Adobe Premiere.

Just WHAT Troubles Them Today? (2020)

An experimental video done entirely within the video game Overwatch. Discussing a unit from my AP Macroeconomics class at the time, everything was recorded from spectator mode in game, with me directing a friend of mine to pantomime as the host as I recorded my voice over the visuals. Recorded in Overwatch and edited in Adobe Premiere.

Confuseral (2019)

An informercial for a fake product based on the novel "1984". Plagued with constant rewrites up until the day before the deadline, I helped direct, film, and edit the final product. Trying to replicate the feel of a "as seen on TV" informercial as well as interlacing it with skits, this project provided me with many firsts. Edited in Adobe Premiere and Adobe After Effects.
